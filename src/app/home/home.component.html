About Us

We are DeeJ and Hades! We are lifelong friends who both have a passion for playing Clash Royale and decided to start streaming. While we may not be the best players, we have many top tier players and pros come on our channel. We also host biweekly tournaments for money. We are always learning, laughing, and having a good time. We usually stream Monday - Thursday around 6PM EST, but join our Discord to always stay updated, or check us out on Twitch or Tik Tok.
